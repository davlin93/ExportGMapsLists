Export Google Maps Lists to GeoJSON

Google Maps currently gives you no option to export place lists to other map apps. This page will take your Google Maps lists and convert them to GeoJSON, which is a format you can import into most mapping apps. Note that there is no way to export the actual label of the place, only the note, so I suggest you put the name in the notes. My use case for this was exporting my list of rock climbing boulders from Google Maps to GaiaGPS.

Instructions

  1. Navigate to Google Maps, click Your Places -> Saved -> click the list you want to export
  2. Note that the name of the place must be in the "Note" section. I haven't found a list where it shows the label.
  3. Copy and paste your Google Maps list into the left box
  4. Click convert
  5. Open Notepad or your favorite editor and paste the GeoJSON from the right box in
  6. Save as .geojson or .json
  7. Import into your preferred map app (tested with GaiaGPS)
