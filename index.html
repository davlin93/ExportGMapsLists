Convert Google Maps Lists to GeoJSON

  1. Navigate to Google Maps, click Your Places -> Saved -> click the list you want to export
  2. Note that the name of the place must be in the "Note" section. I haven't found a list where it shows the label.
  3. Copy and paste your Google Maps list into the left box
  4. Click convert
  5. Open Notepad or your favorite editor and paste the GeoJSON from the right box in
  6. Save as .geojson or .json
  7. Import into your preferred map app (tested with GaiaGPS)
